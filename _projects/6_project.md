This is a rather slow moving project because I am wrapping up all the other topics. Large parts of this work will be joint with Emile Bouaziz.

The idea is to use the T-deformations of vertex algebras from [Project 1](https://arkadijbojko.github.io/webpage/projects/1_project/) and study their general properties and examples. However, the question that needs to be answered first is the construction of appropriate equivariant homology theories for stacks. Once this is achieved, we use an overarching framework of results for such T-deformations to study examples. 

I gave a talk on the recent progress in Andrei Okounkov's Friday Morning seminar - [link](https://simis-my.sharepoint.cn/:v:/p/wangzhenning/IQDCY1YZnOdgR6kMJxGzKEQSAVP0VI7eMU4ObnjTLYAYcYY?e=gMiv1a).
